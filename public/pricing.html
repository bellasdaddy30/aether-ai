Frequently Asked
Is there a free plan?
Yes — all accounts start on a free tier with 5 messages per day and conversation history. No credit card required to sign up.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel from your billing portal at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Secure, encrypted, and never stored on our servers.
Do daily limits reset?
Yes — message and image limits reset at midnight UTC every day.
What is the custom persona prompt?
A system-level instruction you write that shapes AETHER's personality, expertise, and communication style. Give it a role, a name, a field of knowledge — make it truly your own.
